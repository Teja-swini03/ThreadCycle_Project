About Us

At ThreadCycle, we are passionate about reducing waste and making a positive impact on the environment. Our mission is to create a sustainable future by encouraging people to recycle and donate their unused clothing.

Our Mission

To transform how people perceive and manage old clothes by turning them into opportunities for the environment, underprivileged communities, and sustainable businesses.

Our Vision

To build a greener planet by creating a culture of conscious consumption and recycling that benefits society and the Earth.

Our Impact

Since our inception, we have recycled over 5,000 items, supported 2,000 families, and saved approximately 10,000 kg of textile waste from ending up in landfills.
